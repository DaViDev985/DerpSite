F.A.Q's

1)Why derpfest on android 10(Q) provides inlined gapps version?
A)With the lack of official opengapps for android 10(Q) and to prevent a complaints from people breaking their boot by flashing several versions of gapps that are available out in the community we find it as a much more needed necessity to inline our own minimal version of gapps.


2)Will there be a non gapps version?
A)Yes,(Depends upon device maintainer) if he want he can either choose to ship both gapps and gapps less versions. The non-gapps version zips will be named as VANILLA while the gapps version zips will be named as usal zips.

3)Possible to switch between gapps & non gapps versions?
A)It is possible but we never suggest to do it unless you know how to deal with the bugs you might get


THIS QUESTIONS ARE FOR DEVELOPERS ONLY


4)How can i remove gapps from my build?
A)First of all this is not for the end users this is for developer or contributor of the derpfest rom to remove gapps from building run this code before starting to build it


5)How can i apply for official maintainer ship for my device?
A)Contact us on our Telegram group there our lead devs will verify your device and other stuffs and then if they want they will add you (no MTK chip based devices are eligable for officials)
